HR Analytics is based on employee attendance data. 

With the help of Attendance data analysis we are able to uncover Insights like:
	Employee attendance % (Presence in office, WFH and Sick Leave).
	When to schedule Office maintenance work or a Team Building activity.
	Plan infrastructure to reduce the cost.
